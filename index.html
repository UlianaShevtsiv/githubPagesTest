User Login

Description

As a user, I want to be able to login to the Sport News Site, so that I can subscribe to the legue/team news, comment an articles.

Acceptance criteria

When I am viewing any page on the site Then I will see a Login link at the top of the page below And when I click the link Then I will be taken to a Login page with fields for my email and password, and a login button And I will see a link to “Forgot password?” And I will see a button to “Get Started” next to the text “Don’t have an account?” at the top of the page And when I complete the login form and click the Login button Then I will be authenticated against the site member list And when I authenticate successfully Then the login link will be replace by my User Name and I will see a logout link in drop down menu next to my name
